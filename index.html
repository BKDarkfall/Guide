Find out everything about Battleknight!


Currrently battleknight has no real guide and as such the new players tend to suffer. Various communities have their own smaller guides however they are not really avaliable to 'everyone'. Hopefully this site will fix this lingering issue and help the build on its popularity!


How do I use this site?


We have tried to make it as user friendly as possible. First, there is a tour of the game, these pages simply go over through the game quickly and explains where certain things can be found.

After that there are the actual guides, which are split up into Basic, Intermediate and Advanced!

And finally, you can also read the FAQ page, which contains the most frequently asked questions by the players.
